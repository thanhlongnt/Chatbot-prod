UC San Diego Study Participation Invitation

Hello there! You are being invited to participate in a research study titled A study of chatbots to aid debugging.

This study is being done by Michael Coblenz & Thanh-Long Nguyen-Trong from UC San Diego CSE Department. The purpose of this research study is to test the effectiveness of a new debugging tool. If you agree to take part in this study, you will be asked to request debugging help from our tool. Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you can withdraw at any time.

You are free to skip any question that you choose. If you have questions about this project or if you have a research-related problem, you may contact the researcher, Michael Coblenz, mcoblenz@ucsd.edu. If you have any questions concerning your rights as a research subject, you may contact the UC San Diego Office of IRB Administration at irb@ucsd.edu or 858-246-4777.

By participating in this research you are indicating that you are at least 18 years old, have read this information sheet, and agree to participate in this research study. Please keep this information sheet for your records.
